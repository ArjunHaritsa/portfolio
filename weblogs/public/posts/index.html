[First Post] Writing - in the era of AI?

January 7, 2026 General

Why blogging?

Clearly this is an era of AI that we are witnessing with LLMs; simulating & mocking human intelligence as agents - text translation, text interpretation and text transformation is nearly a solved problem by today for an average Joe. Stack overflow is hitting all time low in their listings for any technical assistance post while LLMs are conjugating as senators - from code explanations to code reviews. Their performance is a topic for another day but in this era of “Ask Chat-GPT to create a concrete plan for 3 days road trip in Switzerland”, who has overcome their ADHD to read a blog post?
